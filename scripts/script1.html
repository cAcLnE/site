Blood of Oil

> Ever since a late night 20 years ago, Miriam and Niro Salvich have stressed over a horrifying prophecy
about their daughter, Vasilia, who, as the prophecy says, will be cursed with “blood of oil,” and a “monster deep inside.”
They have kept it a secret from everyone but themselves, and hope to keep it that way,
but keeping secrets could never halt fate’s course. They discover this when Jonas, a young mechanic following
the spread of industrialization and a new suitor to Vasilia, finds himself entwined deeper
in the family’s worries and misunderstandings than any of them could have predicted.
